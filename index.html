Crystals Collector!

You will be given a random number at the start of the game.

There are four crystals below. By clicking on a crystal, you will add a specific number of points to your total score.

You win the game by matching your total score to the randomly-generated number. You lose if your score goes above it.

The value of each crystal is hidden, until you click on it.

Each time the game starts, the game will change the values of the crystals.
